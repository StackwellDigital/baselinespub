About Us

Your neighbourhood spot.

Baseline's Pub is South Surrey's go-to for cold craft beer, great food, and good company. Whether you're in for the game, a night out with the crew, or just a casual pint — pull up a stool and stay a while.

We serve the full kitchen menu right to your table. Just flag your server, order a round and a plate, and we'll take care of the rest.
